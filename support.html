Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is Edgin?

Edgin is an AI-powered ski video analysis app that helps skiers understand technique, movement patterns, and performance using artificial intelligence.

Does Edgin require an internet connection?

Yes. Edgin cannot be used offline.

An active internet connection is required for:
  • User authentication
  • AI video analysis
  • Usage tracking and account features

How do I restore my purchase?

If you previously purchased a subscription or in-app purchase, you can restore it by:

  1. Opening the Edgin app
  2. Going to Settings
  3. Selecting Restore Purchase

Make sure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

If your purchase is not restored after following these steps, please contact support@edginapp.com.

I restored my purchase but features are still locked

Please try the following:

  • Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  • Restart the app
  • Sign out and sign back in

If the issue persists, contact support with your account email.

Why does Edgin ask for my email?

Your email is used for:

  • Account authentication
  • Tracking AI usage limits
  • Providing customer support

Is my data secure?

We use industry-standard security practices and trusted services (such as Firebase) to protect your data. For more details, please review our Privacy Policy.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. If you would like to delete your account and associated data, please email support@edginapp.com from the email address associated with your account.

The app crashed or is not working correctly

If Edgin crashes or behaves unexpectedly:

  • Make sure you are using the latest version of the app
  • Restart your device
  • Check your internet connection

If the problem continues, contact support and include:

  • Your device model
  • iOS version
  • A brief description of the issue

App Store & Subscription Notes

Purchases and subscriptions are managed by Apple through your App Store account. Edgin does not have access to your payment information.

Subscription management, cancellation, and refunds must be handled through Apple.
